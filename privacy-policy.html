Privacy Policy for URL Shortener

Last updated: March 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to URL Shortener ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency regarding how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices related to data collection, usage, and protection when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 ActiveTab Permission: Our Chrome extension requests the "activeTab" permission to enable you to shorten URLs conveniently. We only access the currently active tab's URL for the purpose of URL shortening and do not store or transmit this data.

2.2 Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data, such as the number of times the extension is used and basic statistics related to its functionality. This information helps us improve our service but does not personally identify you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

3.1 URL Shortening: We use the "activeTab" permission solely to shorten the URL of the currently active tab. We do not store this information or use it for any other purpose.

3.2 Usage Analysis: Anonymous usage data is used to analyze the performance and functionality of our Chrome extension, helping us improve user experience.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Chrome extension may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites or services.
